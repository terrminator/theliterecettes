5 Interesting Stock Market Facts

  1. The term blue chip is a prestigious nickname reserved for companies that invest high on the stock market and are nationally known, the name comes from the highest value poker chip, which is blue.
  2. One share entitles the owner of that share to literally a share in the ownership of the company.
  3. Amazon reached nearly a 100% gain at it's highest point in 2018, finishing the year 49% up!
  4. The start of every investment day on the New York Stock Exchange starts with the ringing of an actual bell at 9:30, this has been happening since 1903.
  5. In 1972, a meeting of 24 of the largest merchants resulted in the creation of the market, New York Stock Exchange (NYSE). They would meet outside on Wall Street to invest stocks.
